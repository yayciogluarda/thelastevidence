Hi, my name is Arda.

I started creating crime folders after a night of crime solving with friends. The thrill of unraveling mysteries sparked an interest in me to create engaging and challenging puzzles that would make others think deeply.



My goal is to create something that not only entertains but also makes users engage with the material in a way that requires problem-solving skills. I want my puzzles to challenge your intellect and creativity.



I'm always happy to receive feedback, whether it's about my puzzles or suggestions for new ones. Please feel free to reach out to me!
