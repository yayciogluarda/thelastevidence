Murder of a CEO

When the truth is buried deep, will you uncover it before it’s too late?


Berlin, October 2nd. The launch of Amnolimumab, a groundbreaking drug by the renowned biotech firm RevoluPharma, is overshadowed by a chilling tragedy. Dr. Maximilian Falkenberg, the company’s chief scientist and the face of this revolutionary discovery, collapses mid-speech during the event, leaving the world stunned. His sudden death, shrouded in mystery, sparks whispers of foul play. Was it an accident—or murder?


Step into the shoes of a detective in The Murder of a CEO, an immersive mystery-solving game that challenges your intellect and instincts. Piece together the fragments of a shattered evening as you:


  • Analyze the drink sample for traces of drugs.
  • Compare fingerprints and uncover hidden connections.
  • Hack into a detective's email through a simulated website to access crucial evidence.
  • Recover deleted chats and read encrypted conversations.
  • Match phone numbers to identities and unravel a web of deceit.
  • Eliminate suspects one by one until the true culprit emerges.

Every detail matters, every clue is a piece of the puzzle, and every decision brings you closer to justice—or further from the truth. Are you ready to solve Dr. Falkenberg’s final mystery and unveil the secrets lurking within RevoluPharma?


The Murder of a CEO will test your wits, challenge your perceptions, and push you to the limits of your investigative skills. This is more than a game—it’s a race against time to uncover the truth.


Will you find the murderer before something bad happens?
